Our Origins

SOULMADE was born in Nanchang with a singular vision: to empower creators. We recycle and refine brilliant student designs, transforming them into premium UV transfer stickers that meet international standards.

By handling our own manufacturing and quality control locally, we ensure that every piece of art that leaves our studio is crafted with soul. We are bridging the gap between local campus artistry and the global market.
