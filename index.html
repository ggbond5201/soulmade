Our Origins

SOULMADE was born in Nanchang with a singular vision: to empower creators. We refine brilliant student designs from local art communities, transforming them into premium UV transfer stickers that blend affordability with artistic excellence. Every design tells a story—a piece of soul captured in premium material.

By handling our own manufacturing and quality control locally, we ensure that every piece of art that leaves our studio is crafted with soul. We are bridging the gap between local creatives and the global marketplace, proving that great art doesn't need corporate middlemen. Your purchase directly supports emerging student artists in South China.

From university studios to your collection—this is what SOULMADE stands for: authenticity, quality, and the belief that art has power when it connects directly with those who appreciate it.
